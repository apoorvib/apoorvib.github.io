Astronomy Research

Exosubmoon Stability Model

My research investigated the long-term stability of submoons (moons of moons) by analyzing tidal effects on orbital dynamics. I created this interactive 3D simulation to visualize how different mass ratios and orbital parameters affect submoon survival over astronomical timescales.

Research Highlights:

  • Determined specific mass-ratio thresholds that allow submoons to remain in stable orbits for over 100 million years
  • Conducted high-performance numerical simulations using advanced N-body integrators on Google Cloud Platform
  • Analyzed NASA exoplanet datasets to identify potential real-world candidates
  • Developed this interactive visualization tool to demonstrate stability parameters
